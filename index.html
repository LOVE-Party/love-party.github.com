Hey there! You've arrived at the site of the LÖVE-Party! How good for you!

We are a group dedicated to making fabulous games with the LÖVE game engine.

Here's something we've created...

Fistful of Beef

FoB is a game where you swing bulls to defend yourself against incoming hordes of...other bulls!

It is currently in development for the 18th Ludum Dare — "Enemies as Weapons."

If you have any ideas or if you want to betatest the game, join us at #love-party at irc.freenode.net. We'll be there.

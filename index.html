Here's something we've created...

Fistful of Beef

FoB is a game where you swing bulls to defend yourself against incoming hordes of...other bulls!

It is currently in development for the 18th Ludum Dare — "Enemies as Weapons."

It will feature a single-player survival mode where you have to keep hitting bulls and keep your combo up in order to keep spinning.

The multi-player mode is a competitive cowboy simulator, where dead players will control some bulls.

It is due for release before the deadline of the Ludum Dare, so hopefully it'll be available soon.

If you have any ideas or if you want to beta test the game, join us at #love-party at irc.freenode.net. We'll be there.

Fistful of Beef

FoB is a game where you swing bulls to defend yourself against incoming hordes of...other bulls!

It was developed for the 18th Ludum Dare — "Enemies as Weapons."

Currently it features a single-player mode where your objective is to catch a bull with your lasso, swing it around you and bash those blasted bovine to bits! The quicker you kill, the more points you get.
